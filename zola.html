My role as a product designer

Update this copy. During my time at Zola, we created one of the leading spaces for engaged couples to find and hire their dream team of trusted and talented wedding vendors. I was responsible for creating a simple and seamless experience for both our Vendors and Couples.

Vendor platform

A B2B platform used by wedding vendors, ranging from venues to photographers, to maintain their storefront listing page on Zola, connect with couples through the messaging tool, and set preferences for the types of clients they prefer to work with.

Couple's experience

An experience with education and customizable tools that help couples discover their style, set their budgets, and plan their dream wedding.
