About Me

Hi, there! Thanks for checking out my site! I am currently working on an autonomous delivery robot at Postmates X.

As a bit of a UX generalist, I have a passion for deveoping products that are based in a deep understanding of how people will interact with it and use it. Creating a physical product or user interface that is easy to use and brings joy to people’s lives is my goal.

If you’re interested in designing and testing a user centered product, let’s get in touch!
